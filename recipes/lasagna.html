Lasagna

Delicious Lasagna

Ingredients

  • A box of stouffer's lasagna
  • Italian seasoning
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Ragu spaghetti sauce

Directions

  1. Pre-heat oven
  2. Take lasagna out of box
  3. Put on sturdy baking sheet and place in oven
  4. Set timer for time on box minus 30 minutes
  5. When timer goes off take lasagna out and sprinkle seasoning and parmesan cheese on it
  6. Cook for 30 more minutes
  7. Put ragu sauce in gravy boat and heat up in microwave
  8. Enjoy!

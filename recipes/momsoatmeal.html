Oatmeal

Tasty Oatmeal

Ingredients

  • Oatmeal
  • Oatmeal Water
  • Apple
  • Cinnamon
  • Applesauce
  • Walnuts pieces
  • Cottage Cheese

Directions

  1. Cut up apple into pieces.
  2. Put apple in medium pyrex bowl and sprinkle with Cinnamon.
  3. Put a handful of walnut pieces in the bowl.
  4. Put 1/2 cup of oatmeal in the bowl.
  5. Pour 3/4 cup of oatmeal water in the bowl.
  6. Measure 1/2 cup applesauce and place in bowl.
  7. Microwave mixture for 3 minutes.
  8. Spoon 1/2 cup of cottage cheese on top of oatmeal.

Mac And Cheese

Yummy Mac And Cheese

Ingredients

  • A box of Kraft's Maraoni and Cheese
  • Macaroni Water

Directions

  1. Boil Macaroni Water in a sauce pan.
  2. Open the Mac And Cheese Box...take out cheese packet (very important).
  3. Pour macaroni into the boiling Macaroni Water.
  4. Set timer for 9 minutes.
  5. When timer goes off check to see if macaroni is cooked by spooning a single mac out of the sauce pan. Blow on it so it isn't too hot.
  6. Repeat checking for doneness until done.
  7. Pour sauce pan into strainer and shake it around until all the Macroni Water is gone.
  8. Pour the drained macaroni back into the sauce pan.
  9. With a pair of scissors, cut off the top of the cheese packet.
  10. Pour the cheese packet contents into the sauce pan and stir briskly
  11. Eat out of the sauce pan for that true Black Hawk experience.
